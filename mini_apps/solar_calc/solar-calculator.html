⚡ How to use VDI: Take your VDI number, find the next higher value in a standard VDI-to-AWG wire table, and use that AWG wire gauge. A higher VDI means you need a thicker (lower AWG number) wire. For DC runs under 15 ft, AWG 10 is a common safe choice; for long runs or high current, go thicker.
